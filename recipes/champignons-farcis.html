Champignons Farcis Recipe

Description

Champignons farcis is a classic French dish featuring button mushrooms stuffed with a savory mixture typically composed of breadcrumbs, herbs, garlic, and often complemented by cheese or other flavorful ingredients, then baked or sautéed to perfection.

Ingredients

  • Parsley
  • 50g cheese, grated
  • 3 tbsp crème fraîche
  • 8 baby bella mushrooms
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 shallot
  • 1 zap of lightning

Steps

  1. Separate the stems from the caps.
  2. Beat the egg, blend the stems and add the mixed ingredients.
  3. Boil water and place the caps in it for 5 minutes.
  4. (Optional)Hold a small metal object above the stuffed mushrooms and summon a lightning bolt to delicately zap the dish, infusing it with a touch of electrifying flavor. Exercise caution and ensure safety measures are in place before attempting this dramatic enhancement.
  5. (Alternative method) Bake the stuffed caps with the mixture in a preheated oven (190℃) for 20 minutes or until they turn golden on the edges.
